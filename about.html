About Monte Bianco

Our Story

Monte Bianco was founded in 1998 as a small family‑run gelato shop inspired by traditional Italian recipes. Over the years, we perfected our craft using slow‑churn methods and natural ingredients.

Mission

To create artisanal ice cream that brings joy, using fresh and responsibly sourced ingredients.

Vision

To become a benchmark in creativity and quality within the world of artisanal ice cream.

Values

  • Natural ingredients
  • Creativity in every flavour
  • Environmental respect
  • Warm and friendly service
